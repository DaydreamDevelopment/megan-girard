Who am I & My teaching philosophy.

Hi, I am Megan. Welcome to my site that showcases various aspects of me as a teacher. I am currently a Teacher Candidate at the University of Ottawa- awaiting to start my dream career.

Having an inviting and safe learning environment is something I strive for as a teacher. I feel that school is an opportunity for students to learn, grow and express their opinions and beliefs. Creating a safe and inclusive classroom is essential for student growth and development. Offering various teaching strategies allows students to grasp concepts in a way that best meets their needs. I will always do my best to ensure my students succeed; therefore, providing different opportunities for learning and assessment is imperative. 

Elementary school includes the most important years of an individual's life. Providing students with an environment where they feel safe, allows them to ask questions, seek advice or even just to talk. I want to ensure that whoever I teach and whoever I come across in my school knows that I am there to provide them with the strategies and tools they need in order to succeed in their future. 

“You can teach a student a lesson for a day; but if you can teach him to learn by creating curiosity, he will continue the learning process for as long as he lives” ~Clay P Bedford
